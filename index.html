Dining

At the University of Houston, culinary excitement awaits at every turn! From tantalizing authentic cuisines to cutting-edge delivery robots, our dining options are as diverse as they are delicious. Picture yourself savoring a piping hot latte from Starbucks, indulging in the crispy goodness of Chick-fil-A, or savoring the bold flavors of Panda Express—all conveniently located right on campus. And with a 24/7 dining commons, your cravings are always satisfied, day or night. But that's not all—our student meal plans are designed with flexibility in mind. Whether you prefer unlimited dining adventures or a customizable block meal plan, we've got you covered. And for ultimate convenience, our declining balance plan lets you use your Cougar Cash to feast at any campus eatery. With online menus to tempt your taste buds, the culinary possibilities are endless at UH Dining.
